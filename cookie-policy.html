What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website or use an application. They are widely used to make websites and applications work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and provide information to the owners of the site or app.

Cookies can be "session cookies" (temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (cookies that remain on your device until they expire or you delete them).
